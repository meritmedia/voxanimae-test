THE VOICE OF THE SOUL

We all take a journey of experience and enlightenment throughout our lives. This magazine contains journalistic snapshots of some of those journeys that demonstrate growth through artistic endeavor.

OUR MISSION

The magazine exposes common issues facing everyone and attempts to deal with the subjects through exposure to feelings and efforts from artistic communities, ranging from dance, digital media, painting, illustration, graphic design and music. The art in the magazine is beautiful, the stories intriguing, and sometimes humorous and outspoken. The artists are not located in any particular region but they all share a common thread - the need to voice what is in their souls and to share that with their fellow man.

The readers of Vox Animae are interested in learning about average people going about their lives in above-average ways; people who are making a difference in their own world, and defining a meaningful life for themselves and the people they care about. This magazine knows no socio-economic bounds; it is classless. It is a magazine dedicated to those on their own road of self of discovery.

Interested in exploring the world around them, our readers are innately curious. They want to know who is doing what, and where are they doing it.
